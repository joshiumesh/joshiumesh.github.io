Welcome

Mr. Umesh Chandra Joshi is a seasoned civil service professional with over 35 years of distinguished service in the Government of Uttar Pradesh and the Government of India. He has played a transformative role in public administration, training & development, disaster management, and rural empowerment.

Currently serving as a Master Trainer with the Department of Personnel & Training (DoPT), Mr. Joshi continues to contribute to nation-building through mentoring, capacity building, and knowledge sharing. This portfolio showcases his journey, achievements, and contributions to public service.
