Biography

Mr. Umesh Chandra Joshi is a respected civil servant who began his journey with the Provincial Development Service in 1985. He served in various senior administrative roles across Uttar Pradesh and Uttarakhand, demonstrating leadership in rural development, disaster management, and governance.

Education

He holds a Master of Science (M.Sc.) in Chemistry from Agra University.

Current Role

Currently, he is serving as a Master Trainer under the Department of Personnel & Training (DoPT), Government of India, contributing to the professional development of civil service officers and trainers nationwide.
